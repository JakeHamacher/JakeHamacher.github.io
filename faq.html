Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common mortgage questions.

Minimum credit score requirements vary by loan type. Conventional loans typically require 620+, FHA loans accept scores as low as 580 (or 500 with 10% down), and VA loans often have more flexible requirements. However, higher scores generally qualify for better rates.

Down payment requirements range from 0% (VA loans) to 3.5% (FHA) to 3-20% (conventional). Some first-time buyer programs offer down payment assistance. The ideal amount depends on your financial situation and goals.

From application to closing typically takes 30-45 days. Pre-approval can often be completed in 1-3 days. We work efficiently to meet your timeline while ensuring all requirements are properly met.

Pre-qualification is a quick estimate based on unverified information you provide. Pre-approval involves verification of your financial documents and carries more weight with sellers. We recommend getting pre-approved before house hunting.

Beyond your down payment, expect costs for appraisal, credit report, origination fees, title insurance, and prepaid items like taxes and insurance. We provide a detailed Loan Estimate upfront so you know exactly what to expect.
